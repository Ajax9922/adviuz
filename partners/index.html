ADVIUZ SALES PARTNER AGREEMENT SUMMARY

By applying, you agree to the following key terms of the Adviuz Sales Partner Agreement between you and Instad Web Services Limited (Adviuz):

1. Your Role: You are authorised to pitch, quote (pending admin approval), and close deals as a Sales Partner of Adviuz. All quotes require admin approval before reaching clients. All payments are processed exclusively through Adviuz banking infrastructure.

2. Commission: You earn 10–15% on first orders (based on monthly volume slab) and 5–10% on recurring reorders (based on active client count, last 30 days). Commissions are paid within 7 business days of cleared client payment.

3. Lead Protection: Prospects you register are locked to you for 30–90 days based on stage activity. First-registered wins in all disputes. Matching is done by email, phone, and company name.

4. Compliance: You must comply with CASL and PIPEDA. No unsolicited bulk messaging. All lead data is handled within the Adviuz platform only.

5. Confidentiality: All pricing, client data, and platform information is strictly confidential. This obligation survives termination.

6. Non-Compete: For 24 months after termination, you may not create or advise a directly competing service, nor deal directly with Adviuz-introduced clients.

7. Termination: Either party may terminate with 30 days notice. Immediate termination for material breach (payment diversion, IP theft, spam). Upon termination all commissions on breached activity are forfeit.

Governing Law: Saskatchewan, Canada. Full agreement provided upon approval.
